"Smart Notes" - an application that will allow people, project teams and even companies to make collaborative notes. It means that everyone who is working on the same list is able to edit it, add some more stuff!!! And you can do it on the go, whenever it is comfortable for you, using any of the devices you have: phone, tablet, computer. Isn't it awesome! You can make buying lists, project plans, mutual goals or whatever you want and then add your friends or family to edit these notes together. For example: team startup ideas...
It could become an extremely easy and on the same time effective tool for your everyday needs.

All over the place people are seeking connections and how to make things work comfortable for them. How to use the technology around us in the most effective way. Small and ,for the first time not really recognizable, things are the most important once, because we do them everyday, everyhour and will be doing them always!
On a company level, such notes could solve the problem of collaborative work, could help managers and developers always be with up to date information. Will reduce the amount of time they waste on calls, texts, emails. One of the feature will be version control system, that could save the entire note, from the very beginning up to the end.

FAQ

What will be the price for the app?
The app will be free with limited options. Some extra features for companies mainly will be paid.
What is the release date of the app?
Approximate release date is Spring 2014.
Who is the audience of this application?
The main audience is the day by day users, who are seeking for easy ways of making notes with friends and family. Companies and corporations will be interested as well, to maintain their projects or arrange team building trips.
